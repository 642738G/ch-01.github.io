Instruction

It takes one simple step to publish your own work with this chair! 1. Download an NFC app on your phone to program a NFC tag 2. Take "NFC Tools" for example: Open the app, then click on "write" to add an entry of any type of information you want to encode; save your entry; click on action "write" and tap your phone on the NFC tag you're programming. 3. If you wish to share this chair with someone else and leave the publishing space open, no further action is needed; if you want the information to circulate more privately, click on "lock" or "enter password" do ensure your content cannot be overwritten and will require a passcode to access. Have fun!
